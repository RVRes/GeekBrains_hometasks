LOST QUIVER KILLER SURFBOARD - WHITE

Описание товара

The Quiver Killer is a round tail hybrid surfboard from Lost. With the Short Round template for the nose, the low entry rocker then blends into the round tail which gives this board the ability to be fast on waves but still has the easy paddling and take off.

Характеристики товара

Lost Quiver Killer surfboard
Part of the Short Round range
Outline: Hybrid outline with a large amount of width through the nose for better performance on the wave face
Rocker: Flatter entry rocker but more than the Short Round to keep this board loose in small waves but holds well in bigger waves
Concave: Single to double concave bottom shape
Tail: Round tail for smooth turns and acceleration through turns
The nose and mid section of this board have more width than a standard shortboard to provide more stability
Fin Setup: 5 Fin setup to give you the ability to ride either as a thruster or as a quad
Fin System: FCSII fin boxes
Please Note: This board does not come with fins

Подробное описание

The Quiver Killer works well in small surf with the larger surface area and volume but the tail and nose have been pulled in meaning it gets more aggressive in larger waves with power behind them. The added rocker stops the rails from feeling 'boggy' in larger surf. A great board for all round conditions, feels fun and effortless in small surf whilst precise and forgiving in more powerful waves.

This board comes as a 5 fin setup which means it can be ridden as either a classic 3 fin thruster or as a quad. Please note that fins are sold separately.
